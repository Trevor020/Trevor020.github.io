Cleaning your vinyls

So,you've just started collecting vinyls and you are wandering how to keep them looking shiny and
new or you've been collecting a while and you need some tips. Well, you've came to the right place.
Cleaning your vinyl is rather easy, if you have the right tools on hand. I recommend this cleaning kit
by Kulloomii.
Once you have the cleaning set on hand, simply wipe the dust off using the brush and
then spray the cleaning liquid on the vinyl, being sure to avoid the center. Use the microfiber cloth
to wipe it down and congratulations you just cleaned your vinyl.
Now, just to do it one hundred more times or less, depending on your collection.

Storing your vinlys

Storing your vinyl may seem simple, but there are many ways to store them and keep them from harm.
One of these ways is to keep them in a Vinyl storage case. This ensures your vinyl will stay safe from dust and creasing.
If you want to display your vinyl however, you may want to invest in a wall mount or a display cabinet.
Just make sure if the vinyl is in a display cabinet they are not leaning on eachother too much and there is no friction between them and the sides of your cabinet.

Buying your first vinyl

Buying your first vinyl can be daunting.Do i get an album or a single? Where do I buy them? The most important thing to remember when buying your first vinyl is that it's your collection and that means you control whats in it. Buy music you like or go for the rarest vinyls it does not matter. All that matters is that they are yours. As for where to buy them, there are a great many second hand vinyl stores, new vinyl stores and online shopping. The choice is yours. To easily keep track of your vinyls or buy good quality second hand vinyls, try out discogs and good luck collecting!
